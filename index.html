About Glowforge Interior Design

Glowforge is a partnership focused on clean, modern interior work – especially for gypsum ceilings, lighting and security. We combine practical wiring and installation with creative design so your space looks good and works well.

We work with shop owners, homeowners, hotel managers and office spaces to deliver complete solutions: ceilings, lights, CCTV, smart security, painting and finishes. For bigger projects, we can prepare AI picture designs to help you see how the ceiling, lighting and colours will look before we start.

Request a Custom Quotation

We don’t show fixed prices on this website because every project is different. Instead, we let clients describe what they want. You send us your quotation idea, and we send back a complete quotation with labour and materials separated.

How it works

Simple 3–step quotation process

  • Step 1: Fill the quotation request form on this page: tell us your space size, type (home, shop, hotel), and what you want (ceiling, lights, CCTV, painting, etc.).
  • Step 2: Copy the details and send them to glowforgeinterior@gmail.com or WhatsApp, including photos if possible.
  • Step 3: We calculate and send back a clean quotation: one part for materials, one part for labour, with a total at the end.

If you already have your own item list, you can email it to us and we will price only the labour or the full project as you prefer.
